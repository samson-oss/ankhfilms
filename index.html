About

ANKH

Founded in 2015 by Producer / Director Felex Badza, Ankh Films is a dynamic, award winning full-service commercial and Film production company headquartered in Pretoria, South Africa , known for its ethnic, vibrant, modern aesthetic. We have the combined capabilities of an advertising agency, video production company, and post-production house. From full video and photo production to social media management, media buying and VFX, we are an all inclusive creative company. With over 1 billion views to date on social media and youtube, we have collaborated with brands such as Actorspaces, Showmax, Netflix ,HN Clothing , Sixtymali and fashion labels.
